Profile

Research & Expertise

I am a seasoned statistician with a robust background in biostatistics and data analysis, bringing a wealth of experience from roles in the pharmaceutical industry and academic institutions.

My work has focused on developing innovative tools and methodologies that assist the drug discovery pipeline for cancer immunotherapy — including optimizing target identification using single-cell transcriptomic data for bi-specific antibody design, and developing predictive biomarker discovery frameworks.

I have successfully integrated AI for assisting data-driven target identification and developed a database for the translation of pre-clinical findings to inform dose escalation and safety measures in clinical trials. I develop and deploy Shiny applications and dashboards to automate data analysis and share interactive reports.
